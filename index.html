Welcome :-)

Hello! My name is Chengsong Huang. I am a second year CSE Ph.D. candidate at Washington University in St. Louis,advised by Prof. Jiaxin Huang. Prior to WUSTL, I was an undergraduate student at Fudan University, majoring in Software Engineering.

I study natural language processing and multimodal-pretraining. I'm particularly interested in approaches that help tuning and using large models at lower cost.
